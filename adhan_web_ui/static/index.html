Location and prayer times

Set your home's location once so Adhancron can calculate and update the five daily prayer times for you. Search for a postcode, town, or address, then choose the right result.

Timings are calculated locally from the saved coordinates and update each day. Location searches use OpenStreetMap; you can also enter coordinates directly.

Eid days and takbeer

Choose the locally observed dates for Eid al-Fitr and Eid al-Adha. On either day, Adhancron keeps the normal prayer adhans and additionally repeats the separate takbeer recording during the selected window.
